Redis Meetup & User Group Singapore

 

Redis Geeks, unite!

 

Redis is an in-memory NoSQL database - easy to use, super fast and battle-tested. It's also extremely popular and became the 'Most loved database' in 2017 according to Stackshare. The time is perfect to start a Singapore meetup, let's get together and talk about our experiences, the use cases and how to increase the adoption in this part of the world!
